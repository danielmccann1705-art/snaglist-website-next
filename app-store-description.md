# Snaglist — App Store Description

**Snaglist — The Snagging App Built by a Site Manager**

Capture snags in 5 seconds with one hand. Share with contractors via Magic Links — no download needed. Generate professional PDF reports in one tap. Built by a site manager with 15 years on real construction sites.

**CAPTURE DEFECTS FAST**
- Log snags in seconds with rapid one-handed entry
- Take photos and draw directly on them to pinpoint issues
- Set priority levels (Low, Medium, High, Critical)
- Assign to specific trades with due dates
- Works offline in basements and remote sites — syncs automatically when back online

**SHARE WITH MAGIC LINKS**
- Send snag lists to contractors via a secure link
- No app download required — contractors open it in their browser
- Choose access levels: View, Update, or Full
- PIN protection and expiry dates for security
- Contractors can mark snags complete, add photos, and leave notes
- QR codes for printed handover packs

**PROFESSIONAL PDF REPORTS**
- Generate branded PDF snag reports in one tap
- Include photos, markups, status, priority, and location
- Share with clients, architects, and project stakeholders
- Reports your clients will actually read

**FLOOR PLAN PINS**
- Upload digital floor plans and blueprints
- Drop pins exactly where work is needed
- See all snags visually on each floor
- Tap a pin to jump straight to the snag detail

**TRACK EVERY SNAG TO CLOSE-OUT**
- Real-time status tracking: Open → In Progress → Resolved → Verified
- Filter by project, floor, trade, priority, or status
- Push notifications when contractors complete work
- Progress dashboards showing completion rates

**BUILT FOR THE JOB SITE**
- Offline mode — full functionality without internet
- Cloud sync across all your devices
- One-handed operation for when you're holding a coffee (or a hard hat)
- Designed and tested on real construction sites

**PRICING**
- Free: 1 project, basic snagging tools
- Pro (£14.99/mo): Unlimited projects, PDF exports, photo markup, priority support — 14-day free trial, no credit card required
- Team (£39.99/mo): Admin controls, team roles, API access, SSO

**WHO IT'S FOR**
Site managers, clerks of works, project managers, general contractors, superintendents, quantity surveyors, subcontractors, property developers, and architects.

Whether you call it snagging, punch lists, or defect management — Snaglist fits your workflow.
