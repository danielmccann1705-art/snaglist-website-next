## Problem / Solution Section

### Section Header:
You know the drill.

### The Problem:

**400 messages to find one photo.**

Your plumber sends a blurry photo to the group chat at 6am. Your electrician replies to the wrong thread. The painter says he never saw it. By Friday, you're scrolling back through hundreds of messages trying to piece together what's done and what isn't.

Then you spend your evening copying photos into a spreadsheet — v2_final_FINAL.xlsx — because the client wants a "professional report" by Monday.

Meanwhile, three snags slip through the cracks. Nobody noticed. Nobody's accountable. And handover gets pushed back another week.

### The Solution:

**Snap. Assign. Done.**

With Snaglist, you photograph a defect, mark it up, assign it to the right trade, and move on. Five seconds. One hand. Even in the basement with no signal.

Your contractor gets a Magic Link — not another app to download, not another account to create. They open a link, see exactly what needs fixing with annotated photos, and mark it complete when it's done.

You generate a branded PDF report for your client in one tap. No more spreadsheet formatting at midnight.

> **Annotation:** The problem section uses verbatim customer language ("400 messages," "v2_final_FINAL.xlsx") to create immediate recognition. It escalates from annoying → time-wasting → consequential (delayed handover). The solution mirrors the three steps in three short sentences, creating a sense of relief.

Built for the site, not the office.

### Feature 1: 5-Second Capture
**Headline:** Log a snag before your tea goes cold.
**Body:** Photo, markup, location, trade — captured in one flow. Designed for one-handed use while walking the site. Works on patchy signal because we know what basements are like.

### Feature 2: Magic Links
**Headline:** Your subbie won't download another app. They don't have to.
**Body:** Send contractors a secure link. They see their assigned snags with annotated photos and clear descriptions. No download. No login. No training. No excuses.

### Feature 3: One-Tap PDF Reports
**Headline:** Professional reports without the Sunday night formatting session.
**Body:** Generate branded, client-ready snag list PDFs directly from your phone. Every photo, every status, every note — formatted and ready to send.

**Headline:** Know what's done without asking.
**Body:** Watch snags move from Open to In Progress to Resolved to Verified. No more chasing trades for updates. No more "I thought someone else was doing that."

> **Annotation:** Each feature headline speaks to a specific pain (cold tea = speed, "won't download another app" = contractor resistance, "Sunday night" = report dread, "without asking" = chasing). The body explains the mechanic, but the headline sells the outcome.
